I enjoy the full spectrum of game development, from engineering to design. Mostly, I live on the technical end with <b>game engines and asset creation tools</b>. My energy is especially invested in <b>procedural generation</b> for game contents. I often approach my topics with simulation and deep learning methods.

On the design end, I’m deeply intrigued by the concept of <b>experience engineering</b>. You will find me talking about it at length in my [blogs](/blog). I’m especially interested in finding out how we could potentially teach computers to learn experience generation. I personally believe it could be the next-big-thing in our craft.

As a hobbyist, I love creating <b>concept arts and storytelling</b>. You can browse them in my gallery. They are often presented in forms of paintings, 3D models or manuscripts.

In general life, I'm just a common dude who likes reading, hiking and drinking a LOT of coffee. I enjoy in-depth, small-group interactions a lot more than parties😄.
