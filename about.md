I enjoy the full spectrum of game development, from engineering to design. Mostly, I live on the technical end with <span style="color:#ac4142;">game engines and asset creation tools</span>. My energy is especially invested in <span style="color:#ac4142;">procedural generation</span> for game contents. I often approach my topics with simulation and deep learning methods.

On the design end, I’m deeply intrigued by the concept of <span style="color:#ac4142;">experience engineering</span>. You will find me talking about it at length in my [blogs](/blog). I’m especially interested in finding out how we could potentially teach computers to learn experience generation. I personally believe it could be the next-big-thing in our craft.

As a hobbyist, I love creating concept arts and storytelling. You can browse them in my gallery. They are often presented in forms of paintings, 3D models or manuscripts.

In general life, you will see a common dude who likes reading, hiking and drinking a LOT of coffee. I have also wanted a cat for ages, but stayed allergic to it. Instead, I keep a goldfish called Friedrich.

Well, that’s quite much about me!
